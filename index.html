How it works

Three steps in the Kommutr app. Details on the Ride page.

  1. 1

    Request

    Choose a saved place or set your destination on the map, then confirm trip details.

  2. 2

    Match

    Get connected with an available driver. See status and pricing as the ride updates.

  3. 3

    Ride

    Track the trip, pay in the app, and you’re done.
