Easy three-step booking

Whether you’re a rider or a driver, booking a ride is simple — and done in advance, not on the curb.

  1. 1

    Book ahead

    Request

    Schedule as a driver or rider — morning for an evening commute, or the night before for the AM ride. Sit back; we take care of matching.

  2. 2

    Verified users

    Match

    Get matched with a commuter going your way. Every match is with a verified, background-checked user — so you know who you’re riding with before you go.

  3. 3

    Shared ride

    Ride

    Enjoy your ride. Riders get a lower price; drivers get reimbursed for their expenses.
