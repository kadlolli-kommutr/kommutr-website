Easy three-step booking

Whether you’re a rider or a driver, booking a ride is simple.

  1. 1

    Request

    Book a ride as a driver or rider. Sit back and relax — let us take care of the rest.

  2. 2

    Match

    Get matched with a commuter going your way. Every match is with a verified, background-checked user — so you know who you’re riding with before you go.

  3. 3

    Ride

    Enjoy your ride. Riders get a ride at a lower price; drivers get reimbursed for their expenses.
