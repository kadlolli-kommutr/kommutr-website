How Kommutr works

In the app, riders choose a destination and confirm the ride flow—matching and pricing are handled in the app. Full ride overview →

  1. 1

    Request

    Use Choose a saved place or Set destination on map, then continue in ride booking to enter your trip details.

  2. 2

    Match

    We connect you with an available driver for your route—see status and pricing as your ride updates.

  3. 3

    Ride

    Track your trip and follow your fare in the app—ride, pay, and review when you’re done.
