Easy three-step booking

  1. Request — Book as a driver ahead of time. We take care of matching.
  2. Match — Get matched with a verified rider whose route overlaps yours.
  3. Drive — Enjoy the drive. Get reimbursed for your trip expenses.

What to expect

  • Riders matched near your source and destination
  • Choose 1 or 2 riders per trip — reimbursement increases with 2
  • Expense reimbursement — not opaque “earnings guarantees”
  • You stay in control of your commute schedule
