Drive with Kommutr

Verified riders · Route matching · Expense reimbursement

Share your commute with background-checked riders going your way. Get reimbursed for trip expenses while helping someone get there safely.

Easy three-step booking

  1. Request — Book as a driver. Sit back and relax; we take care of matching.
  2. Match — Get matched with a commuter going your way. Every match is with a verified, background-checked user — so you know who you’re riding with before you go.
  3. Ride — Enjoy the trip. Drivers get reimbursed for their expenses.

What to expect

  • All users are background-checked
  • We try to match riders near your source and destination
  • Choose 1 or 2 riders per trip — reimbursement increases with 2
  • Expense reimbursement — not opaque “earnings guarantees”
